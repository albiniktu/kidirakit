The Monkey Adopted a Tiny Kitten As If It’s Her Very Own

August 29, 2021
| No Comments

Anne Young captured an amazing moment while at the Monkey Forest Park in Bali. This monkey adopted a certain someone as her very own… 

The monkey took the tiny kitten!

No one knows the full story of this kitten and its origins, but it clearly has a protector for life. Seeing how this monkey and kitten not only get along but also love each other with the whole family, is simply breathtaking. Mother Nature is amazing!
